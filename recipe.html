Recipe to fight for

Description

A taco is a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a small hand-sized corn or wheat tortilla topped with a filling. The tortilla is then folded around the filling and eaten by hand.

Ingredients

  • Hard Taco Shells.
  • Soft Corn/Flour Tortillas.
  • Taco Meat (Ground Beef/Pork/Chicken/Seafood)
  • Beans (black beans, refried beans)
  • Shredded Cheese.
  • Lettuce.
  • Onion..
  • Pico de Gallo.

Directions

  1. In 10-inch nonstick skillet, cook beef over medium-high heat 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Stir frequently, until thoroughly cooked,then drain.
  3. Stir in water and taco seasoning mix; heat to boiling.
  4. Reduce heat; simmer uncovered 3 to 4 minutes, stirring frequently, until thickened.
  5. Divide beef among heated shells; top with cheese, lettuce, tomato and sour cream.
